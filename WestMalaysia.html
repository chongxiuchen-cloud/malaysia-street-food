TOP 10 MUST TRY FOOD IN WEST MALAYSIA

Discover the delicious culinary treasures of West Malaysia

Penang Char Kway Teow
★★★★★

Stir-fried rice noodles with prawns, eggs, and chives. A Penang specialty known for its smoky flavor.

Nasi Lemak
★★★★★

Fragrant rice cooked in coconut milk, served with sambal, anchovies, peanuts, and boiled egg.

Hainanese Chicken Rice
★★★★☆

Poached chicken served with fragrant rice cooked in chicken stock. Often accompanied by chili sauce.

Nasi Kandar
★★★★☆

Steamed rice served with a variety of curries and side dishes. Originated from Penang.

Satay
★★★★★

Skewered and grilled meat served with peanut sauce. A popular Malaysian street food.

Asam Laksa
★★★★☆

A tangy, fish-based noodle soup. A Penang specialty with a distinctive sour taste.

Ikan Bakar
★★★★☆

Grilled fish marinated in a spicy sauce. Typically wrapped in banana leaves for extra flavor.

Cendol
★★★★★

Icy dessert with green rice flour jelly, coconut milk, and palm sugar syrup. Refreshing in hot weather.

Bak Kut Teh
★★★★☆

Pork rib soup cooked with herbs and spices. A comforting dish with medicinal properties.

Roti Canai with Teh Tarik
★★★★★

Flaky flatbread served with pulled tea. A classic Malaysian breakfast combination.
